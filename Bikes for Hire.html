Standard Road bikes

A Lightweight entry level bike suitable for the basic needs such as charity rides or work travel. Prices start from £70 per week or £15 per day

Deluxe Road Bike

These bikes are suited for experienced race competitors of both cycling and triathalon. Prices start from £100 per week or £20 per day

Hybrid Bikes

These bikes are for people who want a leisurely stroll around the town or just general sightseeing. Prices start from £50 per week or £10 per day

Deluxe Hybrid Bike

These bikes are very similar to the Hybrid Bike however come equipped with a rear luggage rack and an optional front basket. Prices start from £60 per week or £13 per day

Electric Bikes

These bikes come equipped with a powerful 250 watt for pedal free acceleration ideal for going up hills. Prices start from £150 per week or £40 per day with a refundable deposit of £250 required
